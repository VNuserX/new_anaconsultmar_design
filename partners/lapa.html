Lapa Ltd Training Center

Lapa Ltd is a certified training provider offering a wide range of STCW courses, including basic safety, advanced firefighting, tanker familiarization, and security training. Located in Riga, Latvia, the center is equipped with modern simulators and experienced instructors.

  • STCW Basic Safety Training
  • Advanced Fire Fighting
  • Tanker Familiarization (Oil, Chemical, Gas)
  • Security Awareness & Designated Security Duties
  • Engine Room Simulator Courses
  • Bridge Resource Management
