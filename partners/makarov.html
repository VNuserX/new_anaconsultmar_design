Admiral Makarov University

Admiral Makarov State University of Maritime and Inland Shipping (St. Petersburg) is one of the leading maritime educational institutions in Russia. ANAConsultmar collaborates with the university on cadet training, simulator exercises, and advanced safety courses.

  • Deck & Engine Cadet Programmes
  • Advanced Simulator Training
  • Joint Research on Maritime Safety
  • International Exchange Programmes
  • STCW & Specialised Course Certification
