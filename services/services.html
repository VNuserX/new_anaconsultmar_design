What we provide

Our Expertise

We deliver tailored maritime solutions – from pre‑vetting and QA audits to maintenance, consultancy, and training.

Pre-Vetting

We provide pre-vetting inspection in order to assure that the ship is in compliance with latest VIQ and in proper practical/cosmetic condition.

QA Audits

We provide QA audit in order to assure that the shipboard organization/procedures/performance is in compliance with the company QA System ISO 9000/2015.

Riken Keiki Maintenance

We provide calibration, sensors replacement and other services on board and ashore for Riken Keiki portable gas measuring and detection instruments.

Maritime Consultancy

We provide consultancy on marine personnel supply, maritime training, safety on board, gas carrier cargo operations.

Officers Conferences & Events

We provide assistance in organization, setup and executing of maritime events such as officers conference, fleet days, seminar or meeting.

Instrument Consumables

We together with "Martin Bruusgaard & Co." provide fast supply of spare calibration or span gas, spare sensors, hoses, filters and other for Riken Keiki portable gas measuring and detection instruments in the Baltic sea area. Tubes for "Gastec" pumps also are available.
